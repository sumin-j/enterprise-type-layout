INVESTMENT STRATEGY

look for startups

We look for startups with scalable business models built on strong fundamentals (unit economics and defensibility) and proven early revenue traction.

joint investment

We invest in Seed and Series A startups with a typical ticket ranging between $100k and $500k and capacity for follow-on investments.

diversity

This way, most recently, we’ve focused on Mobility, Digital Health, FinTech, PropTech, and AI-enabled Enterprise Software, but we keep an eye across sectors.

Viktoriya Tigipko ICLUB Global President, TA Ventures Founding Partner

Becoming an ICLUB investor

  1. 1.Discuss your investment strategy with your eegion's ICLUB director.
  2. 2.Complete onboarding and KYC.
  3. 3.Receive investment opportunity one-pager, before our monthly ICLUB event.
  4. 4.Attend ICLUB event and deep-dive call.
  5. 5.Co-invest with TA Ventures.
  6. 6.Exit from the investment together with TA Ventrues
