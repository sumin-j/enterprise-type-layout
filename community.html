ICLUB team

Frequently Asked Questions. Here are some common questions about ICLUB.

What is TA Ventures?
TA Ventures is an early stage globally focused VC firm with 10 years of experience. The firm was founded in 2010 by Viktoriya Tigipko. Since 2010 TA Ventures team has invested in more than 180 startups, which resulted in over 60 exits and 6 IPO’s. ICLUB members have participated in over 50 deals.
How ICLUB and TA Ventures connected/associated?
ICLUB members invest only in startups funded by TA Ventures. TA Ventures find the startup, conduct due-diligence, make an investment decision. The same startups are presented at our monthly ICLUB events where our network of private investors make their individual investment decisions.
What is TA Ventures' investment strategy?
TA Ventures investment strategy is focused on early stage startups from Europe and the States. We are industry agnostic, but focus on verticals in Mobility, Digital Health, Fintech, and Enterprise Software.
How as an ICLUB member I receive investment opportunities?
As an ICLUB member, you are invited to join our events either offline or online, which grant you access to deal flows in which we ourselves invest. You will receive 2-3 investment opportunities, per month.
Which startups have the members already invested?
Our ICLUB members have already made 60+ investments. You can see the full company portfolio in our Portfolio section and read about the progress of our portfolio news in the Insights section, which shares the latest news about our investments.
How can I exit the deal?
We invest together with TA Ventures and we exit together. Our average holding period is 3 years. Since part of TA venture’s strategy is to take a minority share, we can take advantage of the secondaries market, as well as more classical exit options of M&A and IPO.
How to become ICLUB member?
Easy. Our ICLUB director will contact you directly and you will receive a consultation on the options available to you at ICLUB. After which, a membership fee is paid and you gain access to our deal flows, and our global community.
Where are you based?
We have offline representation in London, Monaco, Kyiv, Minsk, Almaty, Dubai, Cyprus and all members are welcome to join online.
